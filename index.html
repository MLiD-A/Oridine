Core Technology

Why Cold Evaporation

Ultrasonic humidifiers aerosolize dissolved minerals into micron-sized white dust that coats surfaces and irritates airways. Cold evaporation changes the physics entirely — only pure water vapor enters your air, while minerals stay behind.

💧

Zero White Dust

Natural evaporation leaves calcium and magnesium ions in the tank. No residue on surfaces — critical for hard water regions covering 83% of Central Asian homes.

🛡️

99.9% Bacteria Barrier

UV-C deep sterilization combined with silver-ion filters eliminates 99.9% of airborne pathogens before they reach your breathing zone. Tested to CNS/GB standards.

🤖

AI Smart Humidity Control

Real-time environmental sensors track humidity and PM2.5 levels, auto-adjusting output to keep your room in the optimal 40–60% humidity range for respiratory wellness.

🌿

HEPA H13 Air Purification

The H3-MAX integrates medical-grade HEPA H13 with activated carbon, capturing 99.97% of particles down to 0.3 microns — humidification and purification in one.
